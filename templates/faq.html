Credit Card Safety FAQs

How can I keep my credit card information safe?

Always keep your credit card in a secure place, never share your PIN, and regularly monitor your account for unauthorized transactions.

What should I do if I suspect fraud on my credit card?

Contact your bank immediately to report the suspicious activity and follow their instructions to secure your account.

Is it safe to use my credit card online?

Yes, but ensure the website is secure (look for HTTPS in the URL) and avoid using public Wi-Fi when making transactions.

What are some common signs of credit card fraud?

Unfamiliar transactions, unexpected declines, and receiving calls or emails about purchases you didn't make are common signs of fraud.
